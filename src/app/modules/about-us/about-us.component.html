Write Awayy is a digital gateway to the happy place of writing, reading, sharing and building a beautiful world of stories.

It gives the children a platform for the expression of all of the incredible ideas and creativity their minds are brimming with. With a voice to express themselves, they develop healthy emotional growth and critical thinking skills, which comes hand-in-hand with an improved school performance.

Writing stories and getting them published seems daunting, but Write Awayy makes it easy. We create titles of specific genres and the children write stories for it (there are prompts and a handbook to help them).

The Reading Room of our website is the cosy corner for the children to read the published stories. Written by children from varied age groups, it has stories for the early readers and the more advanced ones. Reading stories written in a language familiar to them makes the experience engaging for the children as they can relate easily to the plots, characters and perspective of the author.

Sharing the published stories with family, friends and on various social media is both motivating and a process of learning. Getting feedback and reviews encourages the children to further develop their writing skills and view their stories from the reader’s perception.

There’s a spellbinding land of stories out there! Write Awayy is excited to explore it with children, and collaborate with parents to raise writers and readers!
